Mezcal is distilled from the agave plant in Mexico. The name comes from the indigenou Nahuatl word "mexicalli" meaning cooked agave In order to preserve its true origin, mezcal can be only produced in one of the certified Mexican states, with Oaxaca as the leading producer It is traditionally served at room temperature, but it is also a pleasing ingredient in a range of other cocktails Mezcal is a drink for every occasion, but it is much more than this. It preserves tradition and contains a cosmic energy of the sun
