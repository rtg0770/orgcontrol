TOBALÁ This is a tiny, feisty plant that grows under oaks trees on high-altitude slopes. It requires up to fifteen years to mature
CUISHE This palm-tree looking agave is great for blends and takes more than a decade to reach its mature state
ESPADÍN It has gained most notoriety due to the versatility of its growth cycle. It is among the easiest agave to grow and can be cultivated domestically
TEPEXTATE One of the rarest agaves in the wild is Tepextate. It ripens over a quarter century. It grows on cliffs at an altitude of over 1,500 meters. This agave plant yields as much as one bottle per plant
